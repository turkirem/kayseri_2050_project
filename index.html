About Us

This digital gallery is designed to help you see global issues in a multi-dimensional and accessible way. Our goal is to create awareness and inspire thought through the power of art.

Gallery Features

Explore the unique aspects of our gallery.

Awareness Through Art

Each image tells a global problem through the language of art. Art is one of the most powerful universal languages in the world. In this section, global issues are brought to life through art in a multi-dimensional way. Each piece is specially selected to raise awareness of environmental, social, and economic crises. Visitors don’t just observe the issues with the creative power of art; they also touch, feel, and interpret them. Artworks contribute to the awareness process by presenting complex problems with simple and striking visuals. This experience not only provides information but also encourages action by establishing an emotional connection.

Interactive Experience

Discover artworks more deeply with AI-powered interaction. With the power of technology, art pieces communicate dynamically with viewers. Thanks to artificial intelligence and interactive designs, visitors can delve into the depths of artworks, explore different perspectives, and form their own interpretations. The interactions in this section turn passive observers into active participants. Users gain a deeper understanding of global issues by directly interacting with the artworks. Additionally, this experience enriches the art journey with personalized feedback and suggestions, enhancing the learning and awareness process.

Your AI Guide

Every art piece in our gallery comes with an intelligent AI Guide to accompany you. When you hover your mouse over an artwork, the AI guide provides a brief explanation of the piece's subject and the problem it represents. This allows you to instantly grasp the meaning behind the art.

For a deeper interaction, you can click on the artwork to start a conversation with the AI guide. You can ask it questions about the piece, get information on potential solutions, or share your personal thoughts. The AI guide doesn't just provide information; it also guides you on how to take action against these problems.

Membership and Access

Our gallery is completely free and open to everyone. The "Curator: Humanity" project was launched with a mission to raise artistic and technological awareness. For this reason, you can access all the artworks, AI guides, and interactive content on our site without any membership or fee. Our goal is to draw attention to global issues and inspire everyone to take action for a better world.
